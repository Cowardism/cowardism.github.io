    "Cowardists" and "Cowardism" - Newest Words in our Vocabulary

Instead, it seems to glorify the crime due to the symbolism associated with that word – whenever the media reports such horrendous acts as “terrorism”, the terrorist groups seem to take great pride in hearing it. In fact, that word itself serves as propaganda for the terrorist organisations.

    What is the true nature of the crime which we currently refer to as "terrorism"? Is it not the cowardly act of killing of innocent and unarmed civilians – including children? Hence, we propose these new words that better represent the crime: “cowardism” and “cowardist”:

         cowardist:
            noun. Person that kill or injure unarmed civilians as means of coercion.
         cowardism:
            noun. Act of killing or hurting unarmed civilians as means of coercion.

    We are advocating a change in our vocabulary whenever we refer to “terrorism” or “terrorists” – we believe “cowardism” or “cowardists” are more appropriate as:
